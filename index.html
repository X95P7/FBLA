Our Mission

The world is currently developing more and more into a technology-based environment. Nearly everything relies on technology, our computers to our cars, and even to something as simple as an alarm clock. Our goal is to educate people about how these systems work; through our classes and resources we teach all generations the power of programming

The Statistics

Growth

Computer science, the industry associated with programming, is one of the quickest growing industries in the world and therefore are in high demand. Demand for people versatile in computer science is projected to grow by 13% from 2020 to 2030, which is higher than the average carrear growth rate.

Oppurtunity

With the growing demand for people with talent in computer science, there is an opening for success and opportunity that is hard to find elsewhere in the job market. With a median salary of $91,000 in the field of computer science is a great career choice to insure a financial future.

Change

Computer science has the ability to mold the future, looking into the past 20 years there is an explosion of enterprises relying on the art of computer science. Companies such as Apple, Google, and Microsoft are prime demonstrations of the change computer science is capable of making in our world
